Axis will be shown to help describing the figure, e.g. $x$, $y$, $z$ axis as follow.

<oo>
svg 250 100 #fcfcfc fig:example-axis

style lc:#00a ls:6-4 lw:1 lo:0.5
line 10 60 240 60

style lc:#f00 ls:0 lw:2 lo:1 fc:#f00 fo:1
arrow 100 20 150 20

axis 20 25 xy
axis 100 25 yz
axis 180 25 zx
</oo>
